SOUNDTREK 1.0

Software development: Martin Gasser

Soundtrek is an alternative acoustic reality for the walks of Klangmanifeste. For a seamless experience you have to prevent your mobile phone from going to sleep mode:

iOs – Settings > Display & Brightness > Auto-Lock > „never“

Android – Settings > Display > Sleep or Screen Timeout > 30 minutes (or longer)
